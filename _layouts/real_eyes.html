Real Eyes (2024) Camera, projector, computer [video installation: dimensions vary] Real Eyes is a video installation. A projector displays a number of eyes. A hidden camera tracks the location of a visitor's body. As a visitor moves around, the eyes follow them, creating an eerie sense of being watched. This project shows how people are always being surveilled: CCTV cameras, cookies in your web browser, etc. In both the digital and physical worlds, you are never free from non-consensual surveillance.
