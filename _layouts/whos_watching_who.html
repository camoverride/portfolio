Who's Watching Who? is a video portrait project. Portraits of tech CEOs are displayed on a monitor within an ornate picture frame. Hidden in this frame is a camera that tracks viewers' movements. As a viewer moves around, the eyes of the CEO follow them.

We take for granted how every interaction with a technology company, whether it be sending an email, taking a picture on our smartphone, or ordering an item online is tracked, analyzed, and ultimately used to enrich huge corporations that are not accountable to the general public.
