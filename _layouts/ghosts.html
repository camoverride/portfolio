Ghosts (2024) Camera, monitor, computer [portrait: dimensions vary] Ghosts is a video portrait project. A monitor within a picture frame streams video from a hidden camera so that the frame appears to be a mirror. However, everyone who ever peers into this mirror has a video of themselves captured and repeated forever. As more videos are captured, they slowly blend with the others, to create ghostly shadows. This project shows how a person's digital footprint persists long after they log off or disengage with a technology. Corporations are incentivized to cling onto your data at all costs, as your data is the entire basis of their business model.
